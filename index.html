Our Sustainable Process

1

Harvest Banana Fibers

We collect banana stems after fruit harvest - agricultural waste that would otherwise be discarded.

2

Extract Natural Fibers

Natural separation of long, strong fibers from plant material using traditional methods.

3

Create Paper Pulp

Fibers mixed with water to create pulp, using 80% less water than traditional methods.

4

Form Paper Sheets

Pulp spread and pressed to form unique paper sheets with natural texture variations.

5

Solar Drying

Sheets dried naturally using solar energy, eliminating need for energy-intensive machines.

6

Eco-Packaging & Delivery

Paper cut, inspected, and packaged in recycled materials for eco-friendly delivery.
